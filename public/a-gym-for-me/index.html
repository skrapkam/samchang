A Gym for Me
Curated lists of specialized gyms
I love lifting, and I love web development. Wanting to merge these two interests, I created A Gym For Me, a site built in React.
A Gym for Me is a site for athletes by athletes. It contains curated lists of specialized gyms in cities across the United States.
The code that powers A Gym for Me is open source. 🏋️
